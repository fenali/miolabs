4 Teafolio

Local Tea Branding

  • Branding
  • Competitive Analysis
  • User Research
  • Visual Interface Design

Teafolio features a series of packaging design for a small local tea brand, with specical focus on attracting young consumers who enjoy reading their books with a cup of sweet tea. Its core design is inspired by the look and feel of a hardcover book that can be kept for decorative purposes, complemented by side freebies (e.g. special bookmarks with personalized quote) made out of eco-friendly materials.
